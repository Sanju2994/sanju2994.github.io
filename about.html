About Nerd Casa

On the face of it, Nerd Casa is just another collaborative content platform, lots of blogs, articles, tutorials from me and my friends who are doing some amazing work, both personally and professionally. But for me, this website is my little peace mission, It's a way for me to remind people (and myself), that there are things we truly love doing, that there are passions we are dying to chase but choose to let go of every single day. I want to bring together my friends and eventually a lot many people, people who see the world differently, people who don't think life is an equation to be solved, or a process to be followed, or an efficiency graph to be plotted. Like most people of my age, I am just another millenial/ Indian/ female/ engineer trying to carve my place in the world. And I'm not willing to settle for mediocrity. I strongly believe that sometime, somewhere in life, we have all felt truly at home, whether it be in a city, a job or with a person. If you have found your home, your casa, your place in the world- don't let go.

About me

I am a mechanical engineer from IITD, and a lover of beautiful words at heart. Most of who I am comes from my father, and some very nerdy friends. I am a profound lover of literature, language, Europe and music that reminds me of good times. I am also a strongly opinionated and stubborn freak, but that's a story not for the internet.

This, below, is a tribute to my friends, people who have transferred some very important values to me, values that I have come to base my life on, and that define who I am. I love these people from the core of my heart, and 'about me' is incomplete without their special mention.

This is exactly who I was before I met Mr. Patience. He keeps me grounded to earth, acts as my anchor and reminds me everytime I am angry that it's not ok to pull someone's hair out because they accidentaly stepped on my foot.

This friend of mine could write a book about how best to give a fuck. When he starts talking about things he loves or things he would like to change, he makes you feel like there's more meaning in the world than you ever found.

My soul-brother from another mother. I've never seen someone so comfortable in their own skin, so unapologetically being themselves. My partner in art, love and crime, if I had to describe him in a word-it would be 'petrichor'.

This friend contradicts everything I believe in. He teaches me that no matter how much you care, how passionate you are, every once in a while you should sit back and marvel at the meaninglessness of everything you claim is important.
